## CBC Components & Normal Ranges

| Abbreviation | Component Name               | Function                                   | Normal Range            |
|--------------|-----------------------------|--------------------------------------------|-------------------------|
| RBC          | Red Blood Cells (كريات الدم الحمراء)      | Oxygen transport via hemoglobin            | 4.1 – 5.9 x10¹²/L       |
| Hb           | Hemoglobin (الهيموغلوبين)              | Oxygen-binding protein in RBCs             | 12.0 – 17.5 g/dL        |
| HCT          | Hematocrit (الهيماتوكريت)              | Percentage volume of RBCs in blood         | 36% – 53%               |
| WBC          | White Blood Cells (كريات الدم البيضاء)   | Immune defense against pathogens           | 4,000 – 11,000 /µL      |
| PLT          | Platelets (الصفائح الدموية)             | Hemostasis and blood clot formation        | 150,000 – 450,000 /µL   |
| MCV          | Mean Corpuscular Volume                 | Average size of RBCs                       | 80 – 100 fL             |
| MCH          | Mean Corpuscular Hemoglobin             | Avg. amount of Hb per RBC                  | 27 – 33 pg              |
| MCHC         | MCH Concentration                       | Avg. concentration of Hb in RBC volume     | 32 – 36 g/dL            |
| RDW          | Red Cell Distribution Width             | Indicates variation in RBC sizes           | 11.5% – 15.0%           |

---

### Result Interpretation

- **High RBC/Hb/HCT:** Dehydration, chronic hypoxia, Polycythemia Vera.
- **Low RBC/Hb/HCT:** Anemia (iron deficiency, B12/folate deficiency, blood loss, marrow failure).
- **High WBC:** Infection, inflammation, tissue damage, leukemia.
- **Low Platelets (PLT):** Thrombocytopenia (bleeding risk, autoimmune, medication side effects).
- **Normal CBC:** All blood cells within normal range; no current hematologic disease.
